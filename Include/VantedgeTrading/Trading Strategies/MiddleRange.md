## MiddleRange.mqh

    - Waits for entry hour
    - Draws range from highest high to lowest low, from entry hour to lookback bars
    - Calculates the middle of the range and checks whether price is above or below the middle
    - Enters buy if price is above middle, sell if price is below middle
    - Stop loss on the opposite side of the range

### Pairs/Markets

    - USDJPY
